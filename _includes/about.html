About Trellis-Logic


Trellis-Logic is a contract engineering company specializing in Embedded Software and Firmware for Industry and Internet of Things (IoT).

Trellis-Logic is owned by Dan Walkes, who has more than 15 years of embedded software and hardware development experience on commercial product. Dan led firmware development for RDX USB and SATA storage devices which have shipped over 2 million units worldwide. Dan holds a BS in Electrical Engineering from South Dakota State University and an MS in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Colorado at Boulder
